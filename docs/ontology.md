# Graph Ontology

This document defines the canonical graph schema.

---

# Core Node Types

## User

Represents a platform user.

Properties:

- user_id
- username
- country
- join_date

---

## Team

Represents a football club.

Properties:

- team_id
- name
- league_id

---

## League

Football competition.

Properties:

- league_id
- name

---

## Match

Represents a fixture.

Properties:

- fixture_id
- kickoff_time
- home_team_id
- away_team_id

---

## PrivateLeague

User-created leagues.

Properties:

- league_id
- owner_id
- theme

---

## PersonaState

Daily behavioral persona snapshot.

Examples:

- lurker
- analyst
- critic
- superfan

---

## Topic

Discussion topic.

Examples:

- transfer
- injury
- tactics

---

## Sentiment

Emotion classification.

Values:

- positive
- neutral
- negative

---

## Post

User-generated content.

---

## Comment

Reply to posts or discussions.

---

## Conversation

Social chat threads.

---

## Voucher

Reward token.

---

## CoinTransaction

In-app economy transaction.

---

# Graph Constraints

All nodes have unique identifiers.

Example:


CREATE CONSTRAINT user_id_unique
FOR (u:User)
REQUIRE u.user_id IS UNIQUE
